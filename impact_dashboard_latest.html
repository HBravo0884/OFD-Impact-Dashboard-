About This Dashboard

The OFD Impact Dashboard tracks participation in Office of Faculty Development programming at Howard University College of Medicine — including CME sessions, Dean's Dynamic Duo lectures, the APT Process, SWAT Write-A-Thon, and Investigators & Admin meetings — to measure reach, engagement depth, and faculty development trends.

Data Sources

  • Zoom CME Exports — per-meeting participant reports with email, duration, and join/leave events.
  • HUCM Admin Directory — institutional roster linking emails to department, position, rank, and degree.
  • Faculty Master Roster — supplemental data with ethnicity, gender, and additional credentials.

Key Metrics Explained

  • Avg Attendance / Session: Normalizes series with different numbers of events. Reflects per-event draw independent of how often a series meets.
  • Unique Faculty Reached: Total distinct individuals attending at least one session in a series. Measures broad outreach.
  • Engagement Depth: Buckets participants by total sessions attended — shows how many are one-time vs. repeat vs. highly loyal attendees.
  • Department Breadth vs. Depth bubble: Bubbles to the top-right are departments that are both broadly and deeply engaged with OFD programming.

Deduplication Pipeline

  • Pass 1 — Email match: Identical emails merged to one canonical record.
  • Pass 2 — Name normalization: Display names cleaned and fuzzy-matched across spelling variants.
  • Pass 3 — Username-format resolution: firstname.lastname and flastname entries mapped to full names via the directory.
  • Pass 4 — Shared-account detection: Zoom "Person A (Person B)" pattern re-attributed to the actual attendee.
  • Pass 5 — Zero-overlap analysis: Account pairs with shared series but zero session overlap reviewed and merged where confirmed.

Data Exclusions & Assumptions

  • Ghost Logins Filter: Any session with fewer than 5 participants is classified as an administrative test run or outlier and is automatically excluded from aggregate reporting.
  • Faculty Standardization: To ensure uniform reporting, variations of Academic Ranks (e.g., Professor, Instructor, Assistant Professor) are strictly gathered under the single Position label Other Faculty, unless manually stated otherwise.

Data Corrections (Manual Tracking)

Because spelling variations exist across registration platforms, the system pulls an absolute ground-truth file directory_overrides.csv during the final build sequence. Any manual additions to this file permanently fix the specific participant's department, rank, or spelling anomalies across the entire dataset!
